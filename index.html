This website is all about Joshua Day

Hi! My name is Joshua Day. I was born in Mesa and am now living in Chandler. I am majoring in Graphic Information Technology and with a passion for video game design. I am also pursuing the Video Gaming certificate. My 3 favorite things are playing video games, trying new food, and traveling around the world.



Video Games

I like to play ultimate smash bros, Pokemon Go, and bejeweled blitz. Because I like to play video games, I also want to create animation and 3D drawings. I have learned how to do that with Maya and Blender.



Tasting Great Food

I like to try different food. Those delicious croissant were from Neighbor Bakehouse in San Francisco, the big dumplings were from "Do It True" restaurant in Taipei (President George H. W. Bush dined there when he visited Taipei), and the 3rd image was Kobe beef we ate at Kobe Japan.



Travel Around the World

My family and I have traveled to all 7 continents. I love traveling because that is a great way to understand different cultures and try out local food. My favorite places to visit include but not limited to San Francisco, Antarctica, Australia, Japan, Singapore, and Taiwan.
